David Weeks

David Weeks, a dynamic artist from Weeks, New Jersey, brings a rich tapestry of experience to his vibrant oil paintings. Trained in jewelry making and sculpting at Tyler College, David later ventured into the graffiti scene, leaving his mark with striking murals across Atlantic City, Philadelphia, and New York City. Today, he masterfully combines classical techniques and the spirited energy of street art, creating compelling oil paintings that capture the essence of his diverse artistic journey. His work is an invitation to explore the depth and texture of an artist who continually evolves and inspires.
